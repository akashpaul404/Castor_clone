Airport Access

Surya Grand By Castor is conveniently located just 20 minutes from Bagdogra Airport and around 15 minutes from New Jalpaiguri (NJP) Railway Station by car.

Transport

Local taxis and app-based cabs are easily available. For added convenience, airport transfers and private vehicles can be arranged through our concierge team on prior request.

Hotel Policies

To ensure a comfortable and seamless experience for all our guests, we kindly request you to review our standard hotel policies applicable across all Castor Retreat properties.

Check-In & Check-Out

  • Check-In Time: 12:00 PM
  • Check-Out Time: 11:00 AM
  • Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

Booking & Payments

  • Reservations are confirmed upon full/partial advance payment.
  • Valid government-issued photo ID is mandatory for all guests at check-in.
  • We accept major credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking.

Cancellation & Refund

  • Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours before the check-in date to avoid charges.
  • Peak season and group booking policies may vary. Please refer to our Cancellation Policy for full details.

Children & Extra Bed Policy

  • Children under 5 years of age stay complimentary with parents (without extra bed).
  • Extra bed or mattress is available on request at applicable charges.

Guest Conduct

  • Guests are expected to maintain decorum and respect property rules and staff.
  • Smoking is strictly prohibited in non-smoking rooms and indoor public areas.
  • Any damage caused to hotel property will be chargeable.
  • Hotel reserves the right of admission for all guests.

Pet Policy

  • Pets are not allowed at any Castor Retreat property.

Visitors Policy

  • Outside guests are permitted in rooms only upon prior approval from the front desk.
  • All visitors must register at the reception and present valid identification.

The Above mentioned TERMS are NON-NEGOTIABLE.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Where is Surya Grand By Castor located in Siliguri?

Surya Grand By Castor is located on Sevoke Road, close to NJP Railway Station and Bagdogra Airport, making it easily accessible for both business and leisure travelers.

Q. What amenities are offered at the hotel?

The hotel offers well-appointed rooms, complimentary Wi-Fi, in-house dining, banquet halls, 24x7 front desk support, and secure parking.

Q. Does the hotel have a restaurant or room service?

Yes, Surya Grand features a multi-cuisine restaurant with dine-in and room service options, offering both local and international dishes.

Q. Is early check-in or late check-out available?

Early check-in or late check-out requests are subject to availability. Guests are advised to contact the front desk in advance for arrangements.

Q. Are events and banquets hosted at Surya Grand?

Yes, the hotel has versatile banquet spaces ideal for weddings, conferences, and social gatherings with complete event support.
